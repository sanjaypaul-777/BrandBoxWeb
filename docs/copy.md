Confident, sales-driven copy for every page of Zentra, built around the real
product flow: user signs up or connects their Shopify store (or creates one
via our affiliate referral link) → installs the Zentra app → clicks one
button → a fully stocked AI store is built from a pre-made theme + product
pack stored in R2.

**Buttons across the app — keep consistent everywhere they appear:**
- `Connect Shopify →`
- `Build my store →`
- `Continue →`
- `Open your store admin →`
- `Create Shopify account ↗`

**Loading/transition microcopy (avoid a bare spinner with no text):**

- `Almost there...`
- `Just a few more seconds...`

**Error state pattern (always state what succeeded, never a blanket error):**
`We couldn't finish installing your theme. Your products were added successfully. Try again, or contact support if this continues.`

**Confirmation microcopy:**
- `Saved.`
- `Connected.`
- `Store live.`

---

## Copy principles applied throughout (for consistency as you expand)

- **First-person option labels** ("Build my AI store," not "AI store builder") — every choice reads as the user's own intent.
- **Reassurance immediately follows friction** — any step asking extra effort (creating an account, granting permissions) is paired with a one-line reassurance right after.
- **Specific over vague** — "under 60 seconds," "15 products," "[N] store owners" beats "fast" and "many."
- **No dead ends** — every error or empty state includes a clear next action, never just a statement of the problem.
- **Confident, declarative headlines** — "Your store is live," not "Your store might be ready soon."
